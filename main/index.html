Shree Shakthi Mahila Sangha is a women's organization based in Bengaluru, Karnataka. It was established in 1999 and has been actively working for the upliftment and development of women in the city. The organization provides various services to women, including helplines, counseling, legal aid, vocational training, and microfinance. It also runs several initiatives to empower women and create awareness about their rights. Shree Shakthi Mahila Sangha has been recognized for its work in empowering women and has received several awards and accolades.
